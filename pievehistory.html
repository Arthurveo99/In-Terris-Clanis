History of Città della Pieve

Città della Pieve, although today known for its fascinating medieval and Renaissance center, has much older roots, dating back to prehistoric times. The territory where the town stands has been inhabited since prehistoric times, as evidenced by various archaeological findings that reveal the presence of human settlements and the continuous evolution of the populations that lived in the region.
